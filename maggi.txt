=maggi recipe=

1. first thing first, buy a maggi from store.
2. take a pan and put some oil with gas turned to medium flam.
3. chop vegetables like tomato, onion and carrot etc and put them in pan with oil and fry it for some 1-2 minutes.
4. now one bowl of water and put the maggi into it.
5. after 5 minutes maggy is ready to eat.
